# Code of Conduct · 行为准则

## In short · 简而言之

**Be kind. Disagree on substance, not on people. This is a tool for the global biopharma BD community — keep it welcoming for newcomers.**

**保持友善。可以在内容上争论，不要做人身攻击。这是为全球生物医药 BD 社区做的工具——请让新来者感到欢迎。**

---

## Expected behaviour · 我们期望的行为

- Use welcoming, inclusive language. · 使用友善、包容的语言。
- Respect differing viewpoints, especially on methodology. · 尊重不同观点，尤其是方法学层面的分歧。
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully. · 优雅地接受有建设性的批评。
- Focus on what is best for the community and the science. · 以社区与科学的整体利益为先。
- Show empathy toward other community members. · 对其他社区成员保持同理心。

## Unacceptable · 不可接受的行为

- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling. · 人身攻击、侮辱、挑衅。
- Harassment of any kind, public or private. · 任何形式的骚扰，无论公开或私下。
- Publishing others' private information without permission. · 未经允许公开他人隐私。
- Sustained disruption of discussion. · 持续破坏讨论。
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting. · 其他在职业场合不恰当的行为。

## Enforcement · 执行

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

维护者有权移除、编辑或拒绝违反本准则的评论、提交、代码、issue 等。屡次或严重违反将导致临时或永久封禁。

To report a violation, please open a private issue or contact us via the channels listed in [README · Contact](README.md#contact).

如需举报违反准则的行为，请通过 [README · 联系方式](README.md#contact) 提供的渠道告知我们。
